# Website Questionnaire — English

Present each round separately. Wait for complete answers before moving to the next round.

---

## Round 1 — Your Business

1. What is the name of your business or brand?
2. In one sentence: what do you do or offer?
3. Who is your ideal customer? (brief description: who they are, what problem they have, what they're looking for)
4. Why should someone choose you over a competitor? What makes you different?
5. Is there any website you like visually and would use as a reference? (share a link or describe the style)

---

## Round 2 — Visual Style

1. What industry or field do you work in? (e.g., technology, health, education, food & hospitality, professional services, creative, other)
2. Do you have defined brand colors? You can share color names, hex codes (#RRGGBB), or just describe them.
3. Describe your brand's personality in three words. (e.g., professional, warm, innovative, minimal, vibrant, trustworthy)
4. Do you prefer a light design (white/light backgrounds) or a dark design (black/dark backgrounds)?
5. Do you have a logo? Do you have your own photos or images to use on the site?

---

## Round 3 — Content & Sections

1. Which sections do you want on your site? Check all that apply or add your own:
   - Home / Hero
   - About me / About us
   - Services / Products
   - Portfolio / Work samples
   - Testimonials
   - Pricing
   - Blog
   - Contact
   - Other: ___________

2. What is the main message you want to communicate when someone first lands on your site? (the big headline in the hero section)

3. What is your primary call to action? What do you want visitors to do? (e.g., "Book a call", "Request a quote", "Contact me", "Buy now", "Download free")

4. Do you have any customer testimonials or reviews to include? If so, share 1–3 of them with the client's name.

5. What contact information do you want to display? (email, phone, WhatsApp, Instagram, LinkedIn, other social media)

---

## Round 4 — Technical Setup

1. Do you have a custom domain registered? (e.g., yourname.com) — If not, your site will live on a free Vercel URL.
2. What language(s) do you want the website in? (English, Spanish, both)
3. Would you like to include traffic analytics? (Google Analytics or similar — you can just say "yes" or "no")
4. Are there any special features you need? (e.g., contact form, booking system, online store, live chat) — Even if you're not sure it's possible, mention it.

---

*Once you answer Round 4, I'll summarize everything you've told me to confirm before we start designing.*
